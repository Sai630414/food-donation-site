Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate food?

You can donate food by clicking on the "Donor" button and filling out the donation form with details about the food you wish to donate. We welcome donations of any size.

Is there a minimum amount of food required to make a donation?

No, there is no minimum quantity required. Any amount of food you are willing to donate is appreciated and can make a difference.

How do recipients collect the donated food?

Once you submit a donation, recipients can view it and arrange for pickup or delivery based on mutual convenience. Our system connects both parties directly.

Is my donation eligible for tax benefits?

Donations to registered charities may be eligible for tax deductions. Please consult with a tax professional to understand eligibility and required documentation.

Can I track the impact of my donation?

Yes, our platform provides an impact tracker, showing the number of people served by your donations. You can also view the total number of people helped by the community.

About Us

We are a food donation platform committed to connecting restaurants and individuals with NGOs and charities to fight hunger. By working together, we aim to reduce food waste and make a positive impact on communities in need.
